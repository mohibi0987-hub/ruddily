Heikin Ashi
What is Heikin Ashi?
A Japanese charting technique that averages price data to smooth noise and make trends easier to read.
Formula
HA Close = (O+H+L+C) / 4
HA Open = (prev Open + prev Close) / 2
HA High = max(H, HA Open, HA Close)
HA Low = min(L, HA Open, HA Close)
Why traders use it
Trend clarity — same-color candles confirm trend direction clearly.

Noise reduction — minor reversals are smoothed, reducing false exits.

Reversal signals — small body + long wicks signal indecision and possible trend change.

Limitation — averaged prices don't reflect actual market prices. Not ideal for precise entries.
